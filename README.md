#Rosales National High School Website

This website was developed to present important information about Rosales National High School. It provides easy access to school announcements, academic programs, activities, and general school background. The website also serves as an online platform where students, teachers, parents, and visitors can stay updated with school events and achievements while improving communication within the school community.

##Features

-Mission/Vision
-Facilities
-About the RNHS
-Contact Info
